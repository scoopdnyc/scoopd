[Per Se](/restaurant/per-se) is a three-Michelin-star tasting menu restaurant in [Columbus Circle](/neighborhood/columbus-circle). The reservation is hard to get, but the most common reason people fail to get one has nothing to do with competition: they are looking on the wrong platform.

## The platform: Tock

Per Se does not book through Resy. It does not book through OpenTable. Reservations are made exclusively on Tock. If you have searched for Per Se on either of those platforms and found nothing, that is why.

Tock has its own account system. You need a Tock account to book, and you need it before the reservation window opens -- not during. Create your account at exploretock.com now, before you plan to book. Add your credit card. Confirm everything works. On the morning of the drop is not the time to be creating an account.

## The drop

Per Se releases reservations on the first of every month at 10 AM, for two absolute months out. This is a [monthly drop](/blog/rolling-windows-and-monthly-drops). On June 1, August opens. On July 1, September opens. The entire month becomes available at 10 AM and competition is immediate.

Be logged into Tock before 10 AM. Know what date you want. Know how many people you are booking for. When the window opens, act. Browsing costs you time and time costs you the table.

## The two menus

Per Se offers two tasting menus: a 9-course chef's tasting and a 9-course vegetable tasting. Both are $355 per person before wine, supplements, or service. When you book, you are selecting a menu, not just a time. Decide which menu you want before you open Tock on the 1st. Switching mid-booking wastes seconds you do not have.

## The room

Per Se's dining room overlooks Central Park and Columbus Circle. The view is part of the experience. There is no outdoor terrace and no walk-in bar program. You book the tasting menu or you are not here.

## Masa is in the same building

Masa, also a Very Hard reservation, is in the same building -- Deutsche Bank Center at Columbus Circle, formerly Time Warner Center. Masa also books through Tock and also drops on the first of every month at midnight. If you have a Tock account and are already tracking one of these restaurants, you are set up for the other. The guide for [Masa](/blog/how-to-get-a-reservation-at-masa) covers the midnight release and what to expect from that booking process.
