[Eleven Madison Park](/restaurant/eleven-madison-park) is a three-Michelin-star tasting menu restaurant in the [Flatiron](/neighborhood/flatiron) district. Getting a reservation is not about checking the app at random times. The entire inventory structure follows one rule: the first of every month.

## The drop

Reservations release on Resy on the first of every month at 10 AM, for two absolute months out. This is a [monthly drop](/blog/rolling-windows-and-monthly-drops). On June 1, all of August opens at once. On July 1, all of September opens. Every available seat for that month becomes bookable at 10 AM on a single morning, and then it's gone.

There is no rolling window. There is no checking back in two weeks. If you miss the 1st, you are waiting until the 1st of the following month. That is the system. The calendar is the strategy.

Be inside the Resy app before 10 AM. Have your date and party size ready. When the inventory appears, move immediately. Browsing different dates costs you the seat.

## It is Very Hard

Eleven Madison Park is rated Very Hard on Scoopd. That rating means the 1st-of-the-month drop is competitive and fast. Being ready at 10 AM gives you a real chance, but not a guaranteed one. Weekends fill faster than weekdays. If you have any flexibility on date, that flexibility has real value here.

## Plant-based since 2021

One thing to know before you book: Eleven Madison Park has served an entirely plant-based menu since 2021. There are no meat, poultry, or seafood courses. This is not a vegetarian-friendly option -- it is the menu. It is seasonal and changes, so there is no fixed dish list to research. If you are booking for someone who does not know this, make sure they know before they arrive. A tasting menu is not the place for a surprise.

## Tasting menu only

There is no à la carte option. There is no bar with a lighter menu. There is no walk-in path. You book the tasting menu or you do not eat here. That structure is part of why the monthly drop carries so much weight: there is no secondary path if you miss it.

## The calendar is the strategy

At most difficult restaurants, effort and timing spread across multiple tactics -- the drop, the walk-in, the same-day cancellation window, the phone call. Eleven Madison Park collapses that to one moment per month. If you understand that and prepare for it, you are already ahead of most people trying to get in.
